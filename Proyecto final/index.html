Wearable to Prevent Accidents in the Kitchens

Gina Anahi Garcia Muñoz
Ingeniería Biomédica
Octubre, 2023
Arduino Sensores Attiny85

- Resumen -

Wearables are electronic devices worn on the body. These devices can take different forms, including medical devices, clothing, and more. These devices often include sensors, processors, and communications capabilities that monitor, analyze, and transmit personal data. There are different wearables devices: the simple devices such as fitness wristbands that measure steps and hate rate, and also there are complex devices, for example, smartwatches. The design and use of wearables can change depending on their purpose. There are wearables that focus on health or fitness activities, while others are more for communication capabilities and may include AI functions. Most of the wearables collect and analyze real time data (activity levels, biometrics, etc) that provides information to generate a sumary of the person who is wearing the device.

- Introducción -

For this project, we decided to create a wearable to help the people that works in the kitchen, so we design a device capable of measuring the distance between the kitchen staff, temperature and gas within that space in order to reduce the risk of collisions, burns or accidents.

- Conclusiones -

Los sensores son fundamentales en la recopilación de datos para la toma de decisiones en tiempo real, la automatización de procesos y la obtención de información en entornos donde la intervención humana puede ser limitada o riesgosa. En resumen, los sensores son dispositivos esenciales que facilitan la conexión entre el mundo físico y el mundo digital, permitiendo una amplia gama de aplicaciones en diversas industrias y mejorando la eficiencia y la funcionalidad de sistemas y dispositivos.
